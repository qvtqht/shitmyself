Portable and Durable
	All forum data is stored as plain text files, improving durability
	Archives of entire store of forum data can be downloaded by anyone
	The entire forum, or parts of it, can be cloned and re-hosted
	Accounts are portable, and can be used across different forums
	Data is not tied to service provider.

Accessibile
	Hike aims to be accessible to all humans and machines, regardless of hardware, software, and configuration.
	Hike will bend over backwards to accomodate every known and testable browser, with and without JS.
	Support for lovable classics such as Netscape, Explorer, Opera, Mosaic, Lynx.
	If you're using a screen reader, there are accomodations.
	Mouse-free qutebrowser and Vimium users should not have issues.
	If you prefer to use telnet, it will be easier than with most websites.
	Registration is not required to participate.
	Personal information and being human are not required to register.

Transparent
	Everything posted to the community is viewable and verifiable
	Voting logs are transparent and auditable
	Web of trust ensures that untrustworthy votes can be detected
	Meta-moderation is possible by voting on the votes, and so on.
	Voting analysis easily reveals most valuable content

Text-art Friendly
	Text-art is accomodated by switching to a monospace font and preserving whitespace
	To enable text-art mode, just tag an item as "textart".

User-empowering
	User "account", aka the private key, remains in user's possession and control
	Same account can be used on multiple servers, communities, etc., without need for "federation"
	Account access is controlled with encryption, not centrally

Advertising, Spam, and Censorship Resistant
	Avoids becoming a spam and advertising target by making over-growth more difficult.
	User-centric operation allows easy migration in case of unwelcome changes.
