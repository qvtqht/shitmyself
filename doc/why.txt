Portable and Durable
	All forum data is stored as plain text files, improving durability and archivability.
	Archives all data, or just segments, can be downloaded and replicated by anyone.
	Can be cloned and re-hosted, with portable user accounts usable across all copies.
	Data is housed by the service provider without lock-down.

Accessible
	Aims to be accessible to all beings, regardless of hardware, software, or configuration.
	Will bend over backwards to accomodate every known and testable client.
	Support for lovable classics such as Netscape, Internet Explorer, Opera, Mosaic, Lynx.
	Tested with text-based browsers, including Lynx, Links, and w3m.
	JavaScript adds convenience, but is completely optional for all features.
	If you're using a screen reader, there are accomodations.
	Mouse-free users of qutebrowser, Vimium, and similar, are accomodated.
	If you prefer to use telnet, it will be easier than with most websites.
	Registration is not required to participate.
	Personal information and being human are not required to register.

Transparent
	Everything posted to the community is viewable and verifiable
	Voting logs are transparent, auditable, and trustable (Kevin Bacon)
	Meta-moderation is possible by voting on the votes, and so on.
	Best content (and friends) for each user can be found with vote comparison.
	Ballot stuffing and other abuse easily detectable with data analysis.

Art-friendly
	Text-art is accomodated with a monospace font and by preserving whitespace layout
	To enable text-art mode, just tag an item as "textart".

User-empowering
	User "account", aka the private key, remains in user's possession and control.
	Identity can be used across multiple servers, communities, etc., without requiring "federation".

Advertising, Spam, and Censorship Resistant
	Avoids becoming a spam and advertising target by making over-growth more difficult.
	User-centric operation allows easy migration in case of undesirable changes.
